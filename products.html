Tic-Tac-Toe Git repo

The app itself!

My very first project at General Assembly was a front end application with my own rendition of the classic tic-tac-toe. For this application I used Javascript and a good amount of jquery to make everything work. It had its challenges trying to figure out the logic of who wins or not but I feel I added a nice creative touch to that to show you who wins and loses. If you want to give it a shot in your browser just sign up with "dummy data" like (notyourreal@email.com), sign in with the "dummy data" and start your game. Don't worry, you dont have to keep track of finished games. Ther app does that for you. Enjoy!
